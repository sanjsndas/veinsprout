Frequently Asked Questions

Find quick answers to common questions about our products and services.

What's the best way to contact customer service?

For fastest response, please use our contact form above or email support@veinsprout.com with your order number and question. Our customer service team is available Monday-Friday 9am-6pm EST and Saturdays 10am-4pm EST. For order-related issues, please include your order number in your message.

How long does shipping typically take?

Most orders ship within 1-2 business days. Standard shipping takes 3-5 business days within the continental US. Expedited shipping options are available at checkout. International shipping times vary by destination. During peak seasons, processing times may be slightly longer. You'll receive a tracking number via email once your order ships.

Can I visit your location to purchase products?

Our headquarters at 25 Union Square W in New York has a small showroom where you can view some of our products. However, we primarily operate as an online retailer, so we recommend placing orders through our website for the full product selection. Showroom hours are Monday-Friday 10am-4pm by appointment only.

Do you offer discounts for schools or bulk orders?

Yes! We offer special pricing for educational institutions and bulk purchases. Please contact our education team at education@veinsprout.com with details about your needs. We can provide volume discounts, customized kits for classroom use, and curriculum integration support. Most schools qualify for 15-25% discounts on orders of 10+ units.

What's your return policy?

Most unopened items in original condition may be returned within 30 days of delivery for a refund or exchange. Perishable items like seeds and live plants are not returnable. To initiate a return, please contact us for a Return Merchandise Authorization (RMA) number. Original shipping charges are non-refundable unless the return is due to our error.
